# Aigle Phase 2 Direction

## Where We Are (Apr 10, 2026)
- 33 films tracked, 31 with Day 1 data
- Dashboard live: Minggu Ini / Mendatang / Riwayat tabs
- Multi-signal tracking: YouTube, TikTok, Google Trends, NOBAR, Day 1, OW
- First filmmaker (Tumpal) saw the data today
- Milo at ~15% automation, targeting 20% by Apr 16

## What Phase 1 Proved
1. YouTube trailer views correlate with Day 1 adm/show (r=0.704)
2. Single-signal is insufficient — TikTok, NOBAR, PH power, and sentiment all matter
3. Cinema operators (Dias) find the data useful for scheduling decisions
4. Filmmakers (Tumpal, pending response) may find pre-release intelligence useful

## Phase 2: Two Audiences, One Product

**Audience 1: Cinema operators (Dias, SAMs)**
What they need: which films deserve more screens this week.
What we give them: Minggu Ini tab — buzz signals, comparable outcomes, allocation context.
Status: Working. Dias engaged. Iterate based on his feedback.

**Audience 2: Filmmakers / distributors (Tumpal, and others)**
What they need: where does my film stand, what can I do about it.
What we give them: Mendatang tab — signal report, benchmarks, strategy, data nuggets.
Status: First version shipped today. Waiting for Tumpal feedback.

## Phase 2 Priorities (Apr-Jun 2026)

1. **Milo to 20%** — Wednesday pipeline runs autonomously. Monday OW collection. Thursday Day 1 collection. Draft Dias message. Target: reliable by end of April.

2. **Compounding loop** — Every pre-commitment scoring produces a code change. TT_SLEEPER was the first. Need this to happen every week, not just once.

3. **Second outside validation** — One more filmmaker or distributor sees the data. Tumpal is first. Need at least one more by end of April.

4. **Signal stack completion** — Get TikTok + GT for more films. Currently only 16/33 have TikTok data. Target: every new film tracked gets TikTok from Dias on Wednesday.

5. **Sentiment signal** — Dias flagged this (Pelangi had views but negative sentiment). No measurement method yet. Explore.

## What Phase 2 is NOT
- Not a paid product yet
- Not a prediction tool (sinyal, bukan prediksi)
- Not expanding beyond Indonesian theatrical releases
- Not building features nobody asked for

## Success Criteria (by Jun 2026)
- 50+ films tracked
- Milo at 30%+ automation
- 3+ outside validation conversations
- At least 3 compounding learnings encoded into dashboard code
- Dias still using it weekly (retention > acquisition)
